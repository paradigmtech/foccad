Our Growth

FOCCAD has supported Improving health and nutrition status of Children born with HIV, Increase access to HIV combination prevention and testing, Increased retention of women and adolescent girls on ART in Nkhotakota, Salima, Phalombe, Thyolo and Dedza districts this year. We have grown our reach by 160% from 331, 914 beneficiaries in 2021 to 683, 655 in 2022.

2022 Impact Summary

FOCCAD through its three pathway interventions has reached 683,655 people in seven districts in Malawi.
